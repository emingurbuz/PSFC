## Version 1.0
**Initial Release**

### Features
- **Scrum Mastery Flashcards:** Three levels of difficulty (Basic, Intermediate, Advanced) covering Scrum roles, events, and artifacts.
- **Smart Mastery System:** Cards are tracked individually; answering a card correctly three times in a row marks it as "Mastered".
- **Review Mode:** A dedicated section to practice cards that were previously missed, helping you focus on your weak spots.
- **Progress Dashboard:** Track your best scores and last attempt percentages for each level.
- **Modern iOS Tech:** Built entirely with **SwiftUI** and **SwiftData** for a smooth, native experience on iOS 17+.
- **Offline First:** All flashcard data and progress are stored locally on your device.
